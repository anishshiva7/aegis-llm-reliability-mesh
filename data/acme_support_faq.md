## Refund policy

Acme Cloud offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all annual plans. To request
a refund, contact support within 30 days of your purchase date. Refunds are
processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. Monthly
plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled at any time to stop future
charges.

## Pricing and plans

Acme Cloud has three tiers. The Starter plan is 19 dollars per month and
includes 5 projects and 10 GB of storage. The Team plan is 49 dollars per month
and includes unlimited projects, 100 GB of storage, and priority support. The
Enterprise plan has custom pricing and includes a dedicated account manager, a
99.9 percent uptime SLA, and single sign-on.

## Support hours and SLA

Standard support is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm Pacific Time.
Enterprise customers receive 24/7 support with a one-hour response time for
critical incidents. The platform targets 99.9 percent monthly uptime; if uptime
falls below this threshold, Enterprise customers receive service credits.

## Data security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. Acme
Cloud is SOC 2 Type II certified and performs annual third-party penetration
testing. Customers can enable two-factor authentication and configure IP
allow-lists on Team and Enterprise plans.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the billing settings page.
Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period, and you
retain access until then. After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days
before permanent deletion.
